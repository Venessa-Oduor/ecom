About Us


Neel Dairy Limited, often referred to as Neel Dairies, is a dairy processing company in Kenya, the largest economy in the East African Community. The company offers fresh pasteurized milk, cream, butter, yogurt, ghee, and long life milk products in Indian Ocean Islands, East Africa, Rwanda and Burundi.It is one of the best Dairy Company.



Mission


To bring wholesome goodness of pure farm fresh dairy products to every Kenyan home.



Vision


To refresh the world of milk in Kenya with a new appreciation for pure healthy delicious milk from farm to table
